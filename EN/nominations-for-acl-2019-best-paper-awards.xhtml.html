Nominations for ACL 2019 Best Paper Awards

We are delighted to announce the list of papers that have been nominated as candidates for ACL 2019 Best Paper Awards. Among the nominees, we have 28 main session papers (17 long and 11 short papers) and 4 demo papers. The awards will be announced during the conference closing session on Wednesday 31 July (17:50-18:20 at Cavaniglia).

There will be 8 awards in total:

  • The Best Long Paper
  • The Best Short Paper
  • The Best Demo Paper
  • 5 Outstanding Paper awards

The nominated papers are listed below in the order in which they are presented at the conference. We hope you will enjoy the presentations of these great papers, and we look forward to seeing you at the closing session!
